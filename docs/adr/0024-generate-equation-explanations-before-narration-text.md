# Generate Equation Explanations before Narration Text

Equation Explanations are generated reader-facing content attached to existing equation Blocks, not new Blocks. When both Equation Explanations and Narration are enabled for a Document, Narration Eligibility may run before explanations are ready, but Narration Guide and Narration Text generation wait for Equation Explanations so standalone equation Blocks are narrated from their generated explanation content rather than from the raw equation alone. Standalone equation Blocks without Equation Explanations are skipped for Narration instead of receiving a separate equation-specific Narration Preparation tag. This refines ADR-0022: Equation Explanations remain a non-blocking enhancement to Reader View readiness, but they become upstream context for Narration Text when the reader enables both features.
